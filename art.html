Low-Poly Collection

This collection of models are made with as little polygons/ faces as possible. This captures the idea of minimalism in 3D modeling: using as little as you can to communicate a familiar object or character.

Hi-Res Collection

The High-Resolution are 3D models with realism in mind. These models use as many polygons as necessary, and have lighting that attempts to mimmic real-world environments. The materials used to coat the models utilize high-resolution texture images as a source.

Wood Burnings

These physical artworks were designed as vector images in Adobe Illustrator and sent to a laser cutter as a cutting path. The laser then cuts various layers and intensities to burn various shades onto the wood.
